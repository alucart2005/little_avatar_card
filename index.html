Six years after the Na'vi successfully repelled the first human invasion of Pandora, Jake Sully lives as the leader of the Omaticaya clan and forms a family with Neytiri, including their children Neteyam and Lo'ak, their biological daughter Tuk, their adopted daughter Kiri (born from Grace Augustine's inert Na'vi avatar), and a human child named Spider, son of Colonel Miles Quaritch, who was born on Pandora and couldn't be transported to Earth in cryostasis due to his young age.
